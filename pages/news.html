September 25, 2025

World Rabies Day 2025 - A Successful Collaboration!

We are thrilled to share the success of the World Rabies Day event, held on 25th September at Sher-e-Bangla Agricultural University. This amazing event was made possible through the incredible partnership of PHARMA & FIRM, #MSD & #Bengle Overseas Ltd. and #BRC_SAU. A special shout-out to Dr. Samia Khan for her outstanding collaboration in making this event a reality.

The day started with a rally, followed by the inaugural speech and the vaccination campaign launch by the Vice Chancellor, Prof. Dr. Abdul Latif & Faculty Dean Prof. Dr. Jahangir, Sher-e-Bangla Agricultural University. Early in the morning, volunteers were already on the ground, tirelessly vaccinating stray dogs under the guidance of Dr. Rup Kumar. A dedicated team of 100 volunteers worked hard throughout the day, demonstrating commitment and care.

A heartfelt thanks to Advisors Mahmudul Hasan Sikder sir for swiftly securing sponsors for the event T-shirts, and Khalil Ibrahim Sir for excellent direction throughout. After a day of hard work, the volunteers were treated to snacks as a token of appreciation.

July 6, 2025

International Webinar On World Zoonoses Day 2025

We, the Bangladesh Radiantself Community, are thrilled to announce the successful completion of our International Webinar on the occasion of World Zoonoses Day 2025! 🌍

With over 250 live viewers and 114 registered participants, this event was an inspiring gathering of global minds, all united to raise awareness and share knowledge on zoonotic diseases — an issue more relevant now than ever.

A Heartfelt Thank You To:
Our esteemed speakers for sharing their invaluable insights and expertise,
The enthusiastic participants for their active engagement,
And our dedicated organizing team whose tireless effort made this event a true success.
